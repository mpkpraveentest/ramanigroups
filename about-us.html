About Ramani Groups

Driven by Trust.
Powered by Growth.

Ramani Groups is a diversified business group headquartered in Coimbatore, built on customer trust, ethical business practices, operational excellence, and a continuous commitment to growth across automotive and consumer business verticals.

Who We Are

Ramani Groups has grown from a trusted regional enterprise into a multi-vertical business group with a strong presence across Tamil Nadu. With decades of experience, the group has established itself as a respected name in automotive dealerships, mobility-related services, and consumer-focused businesses.

Our journey is shaped by the values of integrity, quality, customer satisfaction, and long-term relationships. Every Ramani business vertical is guided by a common purpose: to deliver dependable products, meaningful service experiences, and lasting value to customers, partners, and employees.

From automotive sales and service to new-age business initiatives, Ramani Groups continues to expand with a people-first mindset and a strong focus on excellence.
